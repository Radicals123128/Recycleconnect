Problem Statement

Many reusable items such as old books, clothes, and electronics go unused or end up in landfills, leading to resource wastage and environmental pollution. At the same time, individuals and businesses seeking affordable second-hand goods or recyclable materials often lack a centralized platform to connect with potential sellers or donors. This gap results in missed opportunities for reuse, recycling, and cost savings, while also hindering sustainability efforts.

Key Features of RecycleConnect

Encourage Recycling

Allow users to donate or sell items such as books, clothes, and electronics.

Promote Sharing and Reuse

Enable users to request or purchase pre-owned items, reducing the demand for new products.

Support Firms and Businesses

Facilitate connections with companies that buy second-hand goods like clothes or recyclables.

Optimize Logistics

Use data analysis to efficiently match buyers, sellers, and recyclers based on proximity and item type.

Track and Reward Contributions

Provide insights into environmental impact and introduce a token system to incentivize participation.
